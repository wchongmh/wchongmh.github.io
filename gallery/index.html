About the SCEBE Award

The School of Computing, Engineering and Built Environment (SCEBE) Prize recognizes:

  • Top-ranked student in Building Services Engineering
  • Excellence in sustainable design projects
  • Outstanding academic performance
  • Contribution to engineering student community
Sponsored by Clancy Consulting, this award highlights the vital industry-academia partnership in developing future engineering leaders.

"This recognition fuels my commitment to merging technical excellence with sustainable innovation in building services engineering."

About This Honor

The HKIE Award recognizes:

  • "Top engineering students from institutions across Hong Kong"
  • Demonstrated excellence in both theoretical and practical engineering
  • Commitment to HKIE's code of professional conduct
  • Potential for leadership in the engineering profession

This recognition holds special significance as it bridges my Hong Kong roots with my international engineering education, witnessed through my mother's proud participation.
